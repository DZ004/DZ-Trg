O Nas

DZ Trg je vodilno podjetje v promociji kakovostnih storitev in izdelkov drugih podjetij. Naša misija je povezati potrošnike s ponudniki, ki nudijo vrhunske rešitve.

DZ Trg is a leading company in promoting high-quality services and products from other businesses. Our mission is to connect consumers with providers offering top-notch solutions.
